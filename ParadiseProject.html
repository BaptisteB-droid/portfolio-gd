Instant Sports Paradise



Summer is here! With family and friends, INSTANT SPORTS takes you overseas, under the sun of a paradise island. Share a multitude of sporty and fun experiences without leaving your home.
Instant Sports Paradise is the second game I worked on during my first internship in the video game industry.

Game Presentation

  • Title : Instant Sports Paradise
  • Studio : Breakfirst Games
  • My role : Level & game designer
  • Genre : Family sports game
  • Platforms : Switch, Playstation, Xbox
  • Game modes : Island exploration, various mini-games

Tools Used

  • Game engine : Unity
  • Scripting langage : Unity C#
  • Documentation : Google Suite, Adobe Suite


My missions

  • Testing the main island and coming up with possible improvements.
    My first mission as a game designer on the project was to playtest and come up with some improvements for the main HUB island of the game. After watchng someone who never played explore, I came up with some arguments and possible improvements to fix the issues with the actual user experience on the HUB.

  • Designing the collectibles.
    For the HUB island the team thought of adding different collectibles to gather in a notebook. I was in charge of designing the distribution and areas to make collecting the bugs, fishes and rocks more engaging and making sure to reward exploration with rare collectibles for players who dared walk out of the main path.
