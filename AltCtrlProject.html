Gambling Paradise : The Escape Call



Gambling Paradise : The Escape Call is a game-jam game developped in a week. Getting inspiration from heist movies the player speaks in a phone to give instructions to his accomplice inside the casino to help him escape.
We submitted this project to the Alt.Control GDC competition and won, we were invited to attendn GDC with our team to promote our project at the event in the exhibitor hall.

My missions

  • Researching alternative controller games.
    I was not familiar with the concept of alternative controller games, so the designers of the project decided to research and learn about the possibilities of alt.ctrl games, Arduino, APIs etc... Before choosing an idea and starting to work on the project.

  • Designing a game around an alternative controller.
    Creating a game using an alternative controller comes with new unique challenge that my team had never encountered before. How to teach the controls, the goals, how to design for accessibility were so many questions we had to answer in development.

  • Managing a GDC booth.
    We attended and won the Alt.Control GDC I was charged with a colleague to set-up and manage the booth for the entire GDC event. We installed the game, which demanded special care since it was an alternative controller, and with help of the organizers had a lot of people trying out our game.
