Kahoot Math Games



Kahoot! Math Games are a product of Kahoot! School software used in classrooms and at home for homework purposes.
The games are designed with learning in mind, helped by Kahoot's Learning designers and every teacher and students willing to help playtest and give feedback on the games. We strive to help students understand and grasp different concepts such as fractions represented on a circle or multiplications shown as arrays and shapes.

My missions

  • Designing solo game modes to make homework more engaging.
    With the help of the learning designer we dove into different syllabuses and planned the different lessons we wanted students to learn in the

  • Creating rational level design tables to speed up levels creation.
    To easily track and plan the creation of levels, their consistency and difficulty, I've created an RLD document. The document helped me create different levels and keep a variety of challenges as well as monitor different parameters and keep track of all the levels I already created and integrated.

  • Playtesting the projects in schools to better our work.
    Working on games made to help children learning it is crucial to have playtests planified with them. Participating in these playtests sessions helped me better understand how my work was understood and played by a primary school student.

  • Using Data analysis to improve my work.
    Kahoot! has access to various data tools and I learned how to analyze and learn from data sets to retrieve the data relevant for my levels and improve my work with the data feedback.
